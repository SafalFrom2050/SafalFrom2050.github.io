## Turn Your Room Into a Stage

Augmented Reality is taking our AI-generated games to the next level. **Velocity Vestige: AR Rhythm** seamlessly blends your real-world environment with high-energy musical gameplay.

### Unique Features

* **Physical Interaction**: Instead of tapping a screen, use your actual hands to strike incoming musical notes.
* **Adaptive Gameplay**: The notes fall through your real-world camera feed, dynamically reacting to your movements.
* **Immersive Visuals**: High-spec phones shine here, effortlessly processing the ML models to track your hands while rendering vibrant neon visuals over your surroundings.

### The Magic of AI

This incredible AR experience was born from the prompt: *"An AR rhythm game that plays with music. The user will use their hands to hit the notes."*
The AI handled the complex WebXR integration, camera feed processing, and logic routing so the creator didn't have to write a single line of code.
